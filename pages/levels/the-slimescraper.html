The Start

The start is all about getting to that second Flippity Bippity (aka: flipper) before the crowd arrives. You make the jumps there and you will be way ahead everyone else.

You can get yourself killed if you fall off the second flipper on the side of the slime and it Flippities while you are falling. Something to keep in mind.

It takes some time for the slime to arrive here so you have about 3-5 attempts to get the shortcut.

The Second Flippity Bippity

While diving onto the Force-Field might seem easier at first flance, it is unpredictable. This is the easiest way to take the shorcut. {% include post/youtube.html id="6OX1qDh33S0" desc="Jump & Dive onto the flipper and then jump on the small slope and jump again" %}
