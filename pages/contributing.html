Contributing

You want to help? Yes please!

Ways to contribute:
  • Text
    • Many sections and/or videos could use some additional text to clarify what is happening (ie timings, ...)
    • Links to interesting posts on reddit relevant to a certain level
    • Links to interesting youtube videos (speedruns, tutorials, ...)
  • Videos
    Videos of shortcuts are ideally in color Movie Star and pattern Lightning, default faceplate with no top/bottom costume. But I'll probably take what I can get :)
    • Any of the missing videos as listed below
    • Any video not with color Movie Star & pattern Lightning are eligable to a replacement
    • Useless Shortcuts: you can get somewhere that's not really useful but still pretty cool?
  • Fancy Art
    If you have some experience in Photoshop or designing websites...
    • The pages are quite dry at the moment
    • There is no header/footer
    • There should be a fancy picture on the top index.html page for each box

Contact

This entire site is open source on Github. You can create tickets there or send me an email.
